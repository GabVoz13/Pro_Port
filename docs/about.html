About

I’m Gabriella Vozar, a Digital Media, Arts, and Technology student focused on building clear, interactive experiences — mixing design, code, and storytelling.

Hi — I’m Gabriella.

I’m a DIGIT (Digital Media, Arts, and Technology) student with a Media Production minor. What I like most about this major is that it isn’t only visual design — it’s also interactive development, research/writing, and building experiences that people can actually use.

My work this semester includes interactive web projects (HTML/CSS/JavaScript), a research-focused Tech Issue resource guide, and experiments with React and Three.js. I’m especially drawn to projects where I can combine creativity with structure: designing the look, but also thinking through navigation, systems, accessibility, and audience.

What I bring

  • Creative problem-solving (visual + technical)
  • Experience balancing multiple projects and deadlines
  • Interest in sports media, storytelling, and interactive design
